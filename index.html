    WE TEACH YOUR CHILD TO CODE!

    Techie Kids Club classes teach students in preschool - third grade the foundations of computer programming. Learning to code helps your child build critical math, reading, and logic skills that support future academic success. Plus, it's a lot of fun!

    View more

    Coding For Kids

    Kids are eager to explore their digital world. Our coaches teach your child how to use programming to understand and control the apps, games and robots they love. Techie Kids Club classes also build a foundation for academic success.

    Why Teach Kids to Code?

    Coding skills reinforce important life skills that are essential to success in kindergarten and beyond.

    Problem-Solving

    Finding + fixing programming bugs encourages flexibility and resilience

    Persistence

    Coding encourages trial and error to find solutions

    Cooperation

    Small group activities provide teamwork practice

    Communication

    Interactive projects enhance oral + written language skills

    Critical Thinking

    Analyzing, evaluating and refining programs leads to new discoveries

    Creativity

    Exploring multiple ideas to achieve a goal
